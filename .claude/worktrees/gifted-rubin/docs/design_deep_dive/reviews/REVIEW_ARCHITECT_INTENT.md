# Senior Architect Review: Intent Alignment & Structural Integrity

**Reviewer:** Senior Systems Architect (Tyr)
**Status:** **PASSED WITH ADDENDUM**

## 1. Intent: The "Docking Station" Metaphor
The transition from a "stateless script collection" to a "Docking Station" is successfully realized through the **Agent Chassis** and **Workspace Manager**. 
- **Validation:** By using Git Worktrees, we have physically manifested the "Dock" metaphor. Agents literally "plug in" to a dedicated physical dimension of the repository.
- **Architect's Note:** This is the most critical structural win. It moves the system from "simulated multi-agent" to "physical multi-agent."

## 2. Intent: The "Super Saiyan" Multiplier
The **Augmentation Bay** and **Swarm Intelligence** provide the necessary force multiplication.
- **Validation:** The separation of **Primary Reasoners** (high-tier models) from **Shadow Swarms** (local micro-agents) ensures that the expensive brain only pays for high-value thought. The "Active Context Dial" gives agents the executive agency required for professional engineering.
- **Architect's Note:** To fully support the "Super Saiyan Blue" state, the **Memory Synthesizer** must be designed as a background **Vector Knowledge Graph**, not just a keyword search. Raw text retrieval will not be enough for high-tier architectural reasoning.

## 3. Structural Risk: The "Signal Soup"
With the introduction of hierarchical swarms and real-time event streams, we risk "Signal Soup"—where the event bus is so noisy that critical signals (Distress) are buried under heartbeats.
- **Recommendation:** The **Signal Corps** must implement **Priority-Weighted Queues**. `DISTRESS` and `CRITICAL` signals must bypass the 500ms packetization buffer and reach the Admiral's TUI with zero-latency interrupt.
